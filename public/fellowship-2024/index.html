    • Unreal Fellowship: Games (Americas)

    I was fortunate to be selected for Unreal Fellowship 2024. This year's is different as it's the first of its kind about games, and I'm super grateful to Epic Games for organizing this fantastic activity. I made many new friends, and the Fellowship opened a whole new world for me: firstly, as a creative person; secondly, as a Technical Art Director in the industry; and last but not least, as a solo developer in the community.

    My Fossil Skater game -- a racing game where you ride dinosaurs who skate with car-shoes, diving into an erupting volcano to win -- is coming to Steam, Epic Games Store, and Itch, very soon -- tentatively this October 2024.
